Ivolary Privacy Policy

Effective date:

Welcome to Ivolary (“App”). This policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Who we are

Ivolary is provided by Antonia Strobl (“we”, “us”). Contact: info.ivolary@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • No account data: The App does not require login or create user accounts.
  • Local app data: Your learning progress, preferences and language choices are stored on your device.
  • Push notifications (optional): If you enable notifications, your device may receive a push token to deliver reminders. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • Advertising: The App integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob).
    Beta notice: During TestFlight/beta, the App uses Google’s test ad units. These are for testing only and are not personalized ads and do not generate revenue.

How we use information

  • Provide core functionality (vocabulary study, progress tracking on device).
  • Send optional reminders if you opt in to notifications.
  • Show ads via AdMob (test ads during beta).

Third‑party services

Google AdMob (Google LLC) may process device information to serve ads. During beta, only test ads are used. For more information, see Google’s privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Data retention

Learning progress is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the App. We do not store your personal data on our servers.

Your choices

  • Notifications: enable/disable in device settings.
  • Ads: in production, you may be able to limit ad personalization via your device ad settings and Google’s controls.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

International transfers

Third‑party providers (e.g., Google) may process data on servers outside your country. See their policies for details.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will update the “Effective date” above and may notify you within the App.
